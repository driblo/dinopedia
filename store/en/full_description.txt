Dinopedia is a calm, ad-free dinosaur encyclopedia and quiz for curious
kids — and the grown-ups who get roped into a debate about which clade
Spinosaurus belongs to.

WHAT'S INSIDE
• A growing catalog of dinosaurs across all the major clades —
  Theropoda, Sauropoda, Ceratopsia, Thyreophora, Ornithopoda,
  Pachycephalosauria, and prehistoric relatives like Mosasauridae
  and Pterosauria.
• Tap any card to see size, diet, period, clade, and a Wikipedia
  summary fetched on demand and cached for offline reading later.
• A five-tier quiz ladder — Hatchling, Juvenile, Adult, Alpha, Legend —
  with three lifelines (50/50, Ask the Pack, Skip), checkpoints that
  lock in your progress, and a quiet bit of confetti when you climb.

DESIGNED FOR KIDS
• No accounts, no ads, no tracking.
• Localised in 25 languages (English, Slovak and Czech hand-translated;
  the rest are scaffolded and being filled in).
• Bottom system bar stays visible — no full-screen takeovers.

OFFLINE-FRIENDLY
Article summaries and images are cached for 30 days, so a long car ride
without signal still works for everything you've opened before.

OPEN SOURCE
Built with Flutter. Open-source licences are listed inside Settings.
If Dinopedia made someone smile, you can support development from the
Settings screen — entirely optional, never nagged.
